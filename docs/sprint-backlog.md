### 6a-1: Recruit Companion
As a player, I can recruit a companion at a station so that I gain a wingman.

### 6a-2: Companion Follow
As a player, my companion follows my ship so that I'm not alone in the void.

### 6a-3: Wingman Commands
As a player, I can issue Attack/Defend/Retreat commands so that I have tactical control over my companion.

### 6a-4: Companion Visuals
As a player, my companion has a distinct visual silhouette based on their faction origin so that they feel unique.

### 6a-5: Companion Survival
As a player, my companions survive when I die and retreat to the station so that death doesn't destroy my crew.

### 6a-6: Companion Save
As a player, my companion roster is saved so that my crew persists between sessions.
